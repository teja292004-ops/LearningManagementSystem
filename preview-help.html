Frequently Asked Questions

How do I access my enrolled courses?
Go to My Learning from the sidebar. All your enrolled courses will appear there with progress tracking.
How do I get a refund?
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. Contact support within 30 days of purchase and we'll process your refund within 5-7 business days.
Can I download course videos?
Course videos are streamed via YouTube. You can watch them anytime online. Offline downloads are not currently supported.
How do I get my certificate?
Complete all lessons and pass the final exam with 70% or above. Your certificate will be automatically generated and available in My Learning.
What is TutorAI?
TutorAI is your personal AI learning assistant. Click the chat bubble in the bottom-right corner to ask questions about your courses, get explanations, or get study tips.
